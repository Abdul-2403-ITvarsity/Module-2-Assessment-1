LOST FOREST

Experience the thrill of adventure as you step into our Lost Forest, a mesmerizing oasis nestled within our zoo grounds. Transport yourself to a world that echoes the majesty and mystery of the Amazon rainforest.

Today's activities:

  • Picnic in Nature : Spread out a blanket under the shade of towering trees and enjoy a leisurely picnic surrounded by the sights and sounds of the forest.
  • Nature Walks : Take a guided or self-guided nature walk along the winding pathways of the Lost Forest.
  • Wildlife Spotting : Keep an eye out for the diverse array of wildlife that calls the Lost Forest home
