MONKEY TRAIL

Step into a world where mischievous monkeys reign supreme, and every twist and turn brings a new surprise.

Today's activities:

  • Monkey Coaster Ride : Strap in and experience the thrill of riding the Monkey Coaster, a roller coaster that twists and turns its way through the heart of the trail.
  • Monkey Watching : Spend time observing the antics of the resident monkeys as they swing, leap, and interact with each other along the trail.
  • Photo Opportunities : Capture memories of your visit by snapping photos of the monkeys, the roller coaster, and the beautiful scenery along the trail.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The monkey trail is to the south of the main entrance
